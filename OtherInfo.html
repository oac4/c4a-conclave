Agent,
There are several other events that have been declassified for your viewing.
Firstly, there will be a Covert Spy Challenge.
This will require you to complete challenges with a fellow spy.
Your scouting skills, mental capability, and physical ability will be put to the test.
Work through the missions, and be the first to complete all the challenges to receive the title of Top Spy.

Secondly, there will be an event deemed 'Assassin'
You will be required to be able to recognize someone in a crowd.
Be careful as you turn every corner and always check your back.
Are you brave enough to eliminate your targets before someone takes you out?
This game will put spy against spy as the whole weekend is fair game, minus designated safe areas.
Last one standing will be crowned the Top Assassin.

The last declassified event is entitled 'Gladiator Joust'
Can you tough it out and be the last one standing?
Your skills will be tested as you compete one on one.
Your strength and balance will be put to the test, are you ready?


In addition to those events, this year's Communication Team will be like none before it!
Our operatives will be undercover all over Conclave gathering intel for the Conclave Newsletter.
Interesting in joining the team? Contact our Section Communications Coordinatior Josh Squirrell at jsquirrell@oa-c4a.org

We are also happy to announce this year's Conclave Ceremonies Evaluations in include the following ceremonies:
Pre-Ordeal, Brotherhood, Meteu's Legend, and Vigil.
These will be evaluated according to the criteria that follows:
Speech (Voice Projection, Pronunciation, Pitch, etc.)
Body Gestures (Hand Movement, Walking, etc.)
Drumming (Voice Projection, Togetherness, etc.)
If you have any questions about the preparation for these ceremonies, you may contact this year's ICE CVC Andrew Rice at Andrew.M.Rice1013@gmail.com.
